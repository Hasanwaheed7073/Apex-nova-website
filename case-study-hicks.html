Mens Grooming | Web Design

HICKS | Men's Salon

Master Your Style.

A sophisticated digital experience for a premium men's salon, focusing heavily on aesthetic appeal and creating a frictionless pathway for online bookings.

warning The Challenge

The luxury grooming space is highly competitive. Hicks needed a digital presence that perfectly matched the premium vibe of their physical salon while overcoming the typical clunky salon booking interfaces that deter potential clients.

We were tasked with designing a platform that exudes sophistication while keeping the conversion funnel (bookings) front and center.

architecture The Approach

Our strategists identified that a dark-mode aesthetic paired with high-contrast, classic typography would best represent the "Hicks" brand identity.

  • check_circle Dark Aesthetic: Establishes a premium, exclusive feel.
  • check_circle Typography: Classic serif headers merged with minimal sans-serif body text.
  • check_circle Conversion Focus: Integrated a minimal-click booking widget natively into the hero area.

The Impact

140% Increase in Online Bookings
32% Decrease in Bounce Rate
No. 1 Local SEO Ranking for "Premium Mens Salon"
